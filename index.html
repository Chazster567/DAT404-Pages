Work

Digital Culture

My work on Digital Culture includes a video exploring the history of Virtual Reality and an audio work exploring the shift from analogue to digital. These pieces were created to test my video and audio editing abilities. The video covers the origin of VR and early forms of immersion and then progresses into more recent ventures into this emerging media format. The audio impersonates the feel of a vinyl record being played and toys with the idea of tricking the listener.

The Kaleidowave

Another part of my work inclues interative pieces of creative coding. The Kaleidowave is a big part of this and despite not being my most complex example it is probably my favourite. The rotating circles base their positions off of the position of the users mouse. This means that as the mouse is moved around the canvas, the circles will create waves and pulses of varying arrays. Have a play around and see what combinations you can come up with. If the script is not showing click here.
